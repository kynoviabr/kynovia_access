## Questions

1. Which screen or step felt slowest?
2. Was the visitor/resident identity clear enough before making a decision?
3. Were allow, deny, and manual review states easy to distinguish?
4. Was the reason for denial visible and understandable?
5. Did any action feel risky or irreversible?
6. Were gate status and command status understandable?
7. What information was missing at the moment of decision?
8. What should be visible without searching?
9. Which workflow would fail during a busy shift?
10. What should be fixed before another pilot run?
